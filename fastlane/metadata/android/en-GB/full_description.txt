Mtihani KCSE is a modern educational app aimed at helping Kenyan students get access to free revision past papers by providing them revision materials for the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Examinations (KCSE). The materials include past KCSE exam papers, Mock KCSE exams from top National schools in Kenya among many other valuable learning materials. Mtihani Revise also aims at turning learning into an interactive process by allowing students engage with each other using a live chat application.
